# About KapiMart

KapiMart is a storefront and order platform built for small retailers. It pulls
your catalog, payments, and customer messages into one dashboard, so running an
online shop does not mean stitching together five different tools.

## Built for the way small shops actually work

Most store software assumes you have a warehouse team and a marketing
department. KapiMart assumes it is just you, or you and a few people. You add a
product once, set a price, and it is ready to sell across your storefront. When
an order comes in, you see the address, the payment status, and a printable
packing slip on the same screen.

## Your AI assistant, on your terms

KapiMart includes an AI assistant that drafts product descriptions, suggests
replies to customer questions, and flags orders that need attention. Every
draft is a starting point: nothing is published until you approve it, and your
brand voice settings keep the wording consistent across every product page.

## Sell anywhere your customers are

Connect a custom domain, share a product link, or embed a buy button on a page
you already run. KapiMart handles the cart, the checkout, and the receipt, and
deposits your earnings on a schedule you choose.
