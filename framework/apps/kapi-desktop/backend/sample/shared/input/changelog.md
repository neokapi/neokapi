# KapiMart Changelog

All notable changes to the KapiMart platform are documented here.

## [3.2.0] — 2026-03-15

### Added

- **One-page checkout** — Complete your purchase in a single streamlined flow
  without navigating between multiple pages.
- **Instant search** — Results appear as you type, with smart suggestions based
  on browsing history and trending products.
- **Shipping to 15 new countries** including Japan, South Korea, and Brazil,
  with express delivery in select regions.
- **Wishlist sharing** — Share your wishlist with friends and family via a
  unique link. Perfect for birthdays and holidays.

### Changed

- Product pages load **40% faster** thanks to optimized images and a new CDN.
- The mobile app has been redesigned with larger touch targets, smoother
  animations, and improved accessibility.
- Order confirmation emails now include estimated delivery dates.

### Fixed

- Discount codes were not applied correctly when combining store credit
  with a percentage-off coupon.
- Duplicate order confirmation emails were sent when the payment gateway
  responded slowly.
- Stock counts on product pages did not update correctly after restocking.

## [3.1.0] — 2026-01-20

### Added

- **Guest checkout** — Purchase without creating an account. You can always
  create one later to track your orders.
- **Product reviews with photos** — Upload images alongside your review to
  help other shoppers make informed decisions.

### Changed

- Improved search relevance for multi-word queries.
- Cart now persists across devices when signed in.

### Fixed

- Fixed an issue where saved addresses were not displayed in the correct order.
- Resolved a layout bug on the product comparison page in Safari.

## [3.0.0] — 2025-11-01

### Added

- Complete platform redesign with a modern, clean interface.
- Dark mode support across all pages.
- Real-time order tracking with map view.
- Multi-currency support for international shoppers.

### Changed

- Migrated from legacy payment processor to Stripe for faster, more reliable
  transactions.
- Redesigned the account settings page for easier navigation.

### Removed

- Retired the legacy mobile site in favor of the responsive web app.
- Removed support for Internet Explorer 11.
