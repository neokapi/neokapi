KapiMart Store Administration Guide
=====================================

Welcome to the KapiMart admin panel. This guide covers the essential tasks
you need to manage your store effectively.

Getting Started
---------------

After signing in to the admin panel, you'll see the dashboard with an overview
of recent orders, revenue, and inventory alerts. The navigation menu on the left
gives you access to all management sections.

Managing Products
-----------------

To add a new product, go to Products > Add New. Fill in the product name,
description, price, and upload at least one image. Products are not visible
to customers until you click "Publish."

To edit an existing product, find it in the product list and click its name.
You can update the price, description, stock quantity, and images at any time.
Changes take effect immediately.

Processing Orders
-----------------

New orders appear in the Orders section with a "Pending" status. Review the
order details, confirm the items are in stock, and mark the order as
"Processing" to begin fulfillment.

Once the package is shipped, update the status to "Shipped" and enter the
tracking number. The customer will receive an automatic notification with
tracking information.

Handling Returns
----------------

Return requests appear in Orders > Returns. Review the reason for the return
and approve or deny the request. Approved returns generate a return shipping
label automatically.

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the returned
item. You can also issue immediate refunds for damaged or incorrect items.

Inventory Management
--------------------

The Inventory section shows current stock levels for all products. Set up
low-stock alerts to receive notifications when a product falls below your
chosen threshold.

You can import and export inventory data using CSV files. This is useful for
bulk updates or syncing with external warehouse systems.

Customer Support
----------------

Customer messages are collected in the Support section. Assign tickets to team
members, add internal notes, and track resolution times. Aim to respond to all
inquiries within 24 hours.

For urgent issues, use the priority flag to escalate tickets. Escalated tickets
appear at the top of the queue for immediate attention.
