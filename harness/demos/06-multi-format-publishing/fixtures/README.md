# Getting started with Taskly

Taskly helps your team turn a messy backlog into a clear plan. This guide gets you
from zero to your first shared board in about five minutes.

## Create your workspace

Sign in, then choose **New workspace**. A workspace is the home for everything your
team works on — boards, cards, and members all live inside it.

## Add your first board

A board is a single project or workflow. Click **New board**, give it a name, and
Taskly creates three columns for you: *To do*, *In progress*, and *Done*.

## Invite your team

Open **Settings** and choose **Invite a team member**. They will get an email with a
link to join your workspace. You can change anyone's role at any time.

> Tip: keep cards small. A card you can finish in a day is easier to track than one
> that drags on for a week.

Happy planning!
