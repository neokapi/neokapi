About KapiMart

Quality products, delivered worldwide since 2020.

Our Story

KapiMart started as a small online shop with a simple mission: make premium products accessible to everyone, everywhere. What began in a garage has grown into a global marketplace serving customers in over 30 countries.

We believe that great products shouldn't come with a great price tag. That's why we work directly with manufacturers to cut out the middlemen and pass the savings on to you.

What We Stand For

  • Quality First — Every product is tested and inspected before it reaches your door.
  • Fair Pricing — No hidden fees, no inflated markups. The price you see is the price you pay.
  • Fast Shipping — Most orders ship within 24 hours, with free delivery on orders over $50.
  • Easy Returns — Changed your mind? Return any item within 30 days, no questions asked.

Meet the Team

Our team of 120 people across three continents works around the clock to bring you the best shopping experience. From product curation to customer support, every team member is passionate about what they do.
