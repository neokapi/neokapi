CloudSync Pro Help Center

Welcome to the CloudSync Pro Help Center. Here you will find answers to common questions and guides to help you get the most out of CloudSync Pro.

Getting Started

To begin using CloudSync Pro, download the application from our website and create an account. The setup wizard will guide you through the initial configuration.

Once installed, CloudSync Pro will automatically detect files in your default sync folder and begin synchronizing them across your devices.

Managing Your Files

You can add or remove folders from synchronization at any time through the Settings panel. Simply navigate to Settings > Sync Folders and select the folders you want to include.

To resolve file conflicts, right-click on the conflicting file and select View Versions. You can then choose which version to keep or merge changes manually.

Security & Privacy

CloudSync Pro uses end-to-end encryption to protect your data. All files are encrypted before leaving your device and can only be decrypted by your authorized devices.

You can enable two-factor authentication for added security. Go to Account Settings > Security to set up two-factor authentication using an authenticator app or SMS.

Troubleshooting

If synchronization is not working, please check your internet connection and ensure the CloudSync Pro service is running. You can restart the service from the system tray icon.

For persistent issues, please contact our support team at support@cloudsync.example.com or visit our community forum.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much storage space do I get?
Personal plans include 100 GB of cloud storage. Business plans include 1 TB per user with the option to purchase additional storage.
Can I share files with people who do not have CloudSync Pro?
Yes! You can generate shareable links for any file or folder. Recipients can view and download files without needing a CloudSync Pro account.
What happens if I exceed my storage limit?
You will receive a notification when you reach 90% of your storage limit. Once the limit is reached, new files will not be synchronized until you free up space or upgrade your plan.
